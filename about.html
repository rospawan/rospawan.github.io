Ali Rospawan

PhD Researcher · Electrical Engineer · Lecturer · Lifelong Learner

I'm an electrical engineer and PhD researcher at National Chung Hsing University, Taiwan, with over six years of industry experience in PLC programming, industrial automation, and machinery design.

My research sits at the intersection of advanced control systems, AI-driven fuzzy neural networks, and autonomous mobile robots — spanning industrial applications like extrusion barrels and semiconductor heating ovens, as well as visual SLAM and advanced localization methods for mobile robotic platforms.
