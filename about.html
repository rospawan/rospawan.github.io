Ali Rospawan

PhD Researcher · Electrical Engineer · Lecturer

I'm an electrical engineer and PhD researcher at National Chung Hsing University, Taiwan, with over six years of industry experience in PLC programming, industrial automation, and machinery design.

My research sits at the intersection of advanced control systems, AI-driven fuzzy neural networks, and industrial applications — from extrusion barrels in plastic injection molding to semiconductor heating ovens.

About Me

A Little More About Me

I'm currently pursuing my PhD in Electrical Engineering at National Chung Hsing University under Prof. Ching-Chih Tsai, while also serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Electronics Engineering at Politeknik Manufaktur Negeri Bangka Belitung in Indonesia, where I teach Electronic Circuit Applications and Robotics.

Before returning to academia, I spent over six years in industry as an Electrical Design and Automation Engineer, designing and troubleshooting automation systems for manufacturing. That hands-on background shapes how I approach research today — I'm drawn to control problems that have real, deployable industrial value.

Outside the lab I serve as Chair of the IEEE National Chung Hsing University Student Branch and the IEEE SMC Society NCHU Chapter, and as Secretary of the Chinese Institute of Electrical Engineering Taichung Chapter.
