Research Focus

Our Research Highlights

Monolithic 3D Electronics & Innovations

Developing advanced processing techniques for monolithic 3D integration and BEOL applications. We bridge academic discovery with industrial manufacturability through industrial collaborations.

Semiconductor Physics in 2D Limits

Exploring fundamental physics at ultimate thickness limits. We focus on carrier dynamics, exciton behavior, and novel transport mechanisms in scaled systems beyond conventional limitations.
